##  Project Overview
[Wings & Go is a modern fast-food brand specializing in premium fried chicken with bold flavors and high-quality ingredients. The project focuses on developing a strong and cohesive visual identity that reflects speed, energy, and reliability while enhancing brand recognition. The final outcome will be presented through a complete branding system including logo design, packaging, color palette, typography, and promotional mockups.]

---

## Project Objectives
- [Create a distinctive and memorable logo that reflects the brand’s identity.]  
- [Develop a cohesive visual system including colors, typography, and graphic elements.]  
- [Design attractive and functional packaging that aligns with the brand personality.]  
- [Establish a strong and modern brand presence suitable for marketing and promotional use.]
